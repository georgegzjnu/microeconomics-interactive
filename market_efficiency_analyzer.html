Market Efficiency Analyzer

Interactive tool to explore consumer surplus, producer surplus, deadweight loss, and market efficiency under different policy scenarios. 交互式工具，探索不同政策场景下的消费者剩余、生产者剩余、无谓损失和市场效率。

How to Use

  • Select a scenario to load preset policy parameters (carbon tax, minimum wage, etc.)
  • Adjust sliders to change tax rates, subsidy amounts, or price control levels
  • Observe real‑time changes in the supply‑demand chart and efficiency metrics
  • Data is recorded anonymously to help improve learning materials (no personal information collected)
